About Trees for Tomorrow

The Trees for Tomorrow program provides free trees to homes, businesses, schools, and parks in West Sacramento neighborhoods with a low tree canopy.
In partnership with the City of West Sacramento and with help from community members and volunteers, we will plant and care for 1,000 new trees.

If you live in the project area, you can involved by:

  • Planting a free tree at your home
  • Organizing a tree planting event in your neighborhood, local school, or park
  • Volunteering at a tree planting event
  • Attending a free tree workshop
  • Donating to Trees for Tomorrow

Did You Know?

Trees can do amazing things!

  • Lower energy bills by shading your home or business
  • Increase property values
  • Protect us from summertime heat
  • Keep waterways clean by filtering storm runoff
  • Provide shelter for urban wildlife
